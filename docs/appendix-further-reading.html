Appendix Further Reading

Diagnostics

  • When Can you Safely Ignore Multicollinearity?

Interaction Terms

  • If you center your variables, you can interpret the main effects with an interaction (Schielzeth 2010)

Adjusted P-values

  • Use Benjamini-Hochberg (False Discovery Rate, FDR) for P-value adjustment, not Bonferroni (Nakagawa 2004)

Mixed Models

  • Use random slopes when you can (Schielzeth and Forstmeier 2009)
  • The MuMIn package calculates R2 for mixed models Nakagawa, Johnson, and Schielzeth (2017)
  • lmerTest package calculates P-values based on Satterthwaite’s method for approximating degrees of freedom for t and F tests (Kuznetsova, Brockhoff, and Christensen 2017)
